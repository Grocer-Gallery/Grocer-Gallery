F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are there any charges for registration?

    No. Registration on Grocer Gallery is absolutely free.

  • How do I register?

    You can register by clicking on the "Sign Up" link at the top right corner of the homepage. Please provide the information in the form that appears.

  • What is the minimum order for delivery?

    There is no minimum order for delivery.

  • How do I change the delivery info (address to which I want products delivered)?

    You can change your delivery address on our website once you log into your account. Click on "My Account" at the top right hand corner and go to the "Update My Profile" section to change your delivery address.

  • How do I reset my password?

    You need to enter your email address on the Login page and click on forgot password. An email with a reset password will be sent to your email address. With this, you can change your password.

  • Will someone inform me if my order delivery gets delayed?

    In case of a delay, Grocer Gallery will keep you updated about your delivery through message.
